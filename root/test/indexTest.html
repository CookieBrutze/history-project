Why Simple Design Matters

A minimalist design helps reduce distractions and focuses the reader's attention on the content. Consistent use of spacing, colors, and font sizes can make a huge difference in how comfortable it is to read a page.

This second section is styled slightly differently, with a new background color and header style to differentiate it from the first one.
